# Project Brief: Deep-Tech Personal Portfolio

## Project Overview
A high-fidelity, visually striking personal portfolio website for a student specializing in the intersection of Electrical Engineering (EE) and Computer Science (CS). The site is designed to function like a deep-tech startup landing page, showcasing technical depth in hardware (IoT), full-stack software, and complex system simulations.

## Primary Objectives
- **Impress Recruiters:** Demonstrate "builder" capability through high-impact project presentations.
- **Showcase Technical Depth:** Highlight the ability to manage full-system integration (hardware → backend → UI).
- **Establish Brand Identity:** Position the candidate as a systems architect capable of handling both low-level hardware and high-level software.

## Target Audience
- Technical recruiters at AI, robotics, and deep-tech startups.
- Engineering managers looking for interns/new grads with hardware/software crossover skills.
- Internship reviewers evaluating technical execution and complexity.

## Visual Identity & Vibe
- **Aesthetic:** "Funded AI Startup" — Bold, futuristic, and high-tech.
- **Color Palette:** Default Dark Mode (#0E0E0F) with glowing "cyber" accents (Cyan #00F0FF, Purple, or Neon Green).
- **Typography:** Modern, technical sans-serif (e.g., Space Grotesk) with monospace accents for data/technical details.
- **Animations:** High-quality, purposeful motion (staggered fades, subtle grid backgrounds, and interactive hover states) to communicate sophistication without being "gimmicky."

## Core Features & Layout

### 1. Hero Section (The Hook)
- **Tagline:** "Building intelligent systems at the intersection of hardware and software."
- **Visuals:** Subtle particle or grid-based animated background.
- **CTA:** "View Projects" (Primary) and "Contact" (Secondary).

### 2. About: The "Builder" Identity
- A sharp, narrative-driven section focusing on engineering systems rather than just "writing code."
- Emphasizes the EE + CS synergy.

### 3. Featured Projects (The Centerpiece)
- **Project 1: Smart Garden IoT**
  - Focus: Hardware integration, computer vision (OpenCV), and Flask automation.
  - Highlights: ESP32, Arduino, sensors, and real-time control.
- **Project 2: QHSS City (Multiplayer Simulation Engine)**
  - Focus: Complex state management, real-time sync, and custom scripting APIs.
  - Highlights: Vanilla JS simulation engine, Firebase Realtime DB, and multi-mode architecture.

### 4. Technical Stack & Timeline
- Visual categorization of skills (Hardware, Software, Tools).
- A clean, professional timeline of experience focusing on systems built.

### 5. Contact
- Minimalist form or direct contact links.
- Closing Statement: "I’m always building—let’s create something impactful."

## Technical Requirements
- **Frameworks:** None (Vanilla HTML, CSS, JavaScript) for maximum performance and portability.
- **Performance:** Optimized for fast load times and smooth 60fps animations.
- **Responsiveness:** Fully responsive design for desktop and mobile devices.
- **Code Quality:** Clean, modular, and easy-to-extend structure.

## Success Metrics
- Average session duration (indicating recruiter engagement with project details).
- Direct inquiries via contact form or LinkedIn.
- Successful demonstration of "full-system" thinking during interview discussions.
